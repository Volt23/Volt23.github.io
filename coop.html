Future food district
Visual storytelling of markets

2015

Illustrations for the Future food district pavillion of Coop at the Expo 2015. Dotdotdot, that curated part of the design and the technology assets inside the pavillion, wanted a fast solution for the entrance of the future food district and asked us to illustrate the evolution of markets through 6 milestones. We delivered the work in one week, working aside Lucio Lapietra that has animated the scenes.

Illustration made in collaboration with: Lorenzo Tempesti and Cecilia Negri
